About us

ELON Motors is a Mechanical Engineering based startup that focuses on solving real life problems through its products.

We are an astounding team of engineers with the passion for Innovation and are alumnus of SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram. The collective efforts of passionate engineers, with their vigorous pursuit of developing and marketing cutting edge solutions is responsible for this company formation.



Key areas we are currently focusing are

Student level Motorsport challenges.

Skill Development through unique trainings

Sustainable Mobility Products

Motorsports vehicle parts manufacturing



Mission: We are highly committed to supply quality products at affordable costs with cutting edge solutions.

Vision: To make this world more and more sustainable to live
